# What Is Carror OS?

> **Carror OS is the AI Native Developer Operating System.**
>
> It turns AI coding from vibe-driven into evidence-driven.

---

## The Problem

When you use AI to write code, the same disasters happen again and again:

- Long conversations degrade -- after 20+ rounds, the AI starts hallucinating and deleting working code.
- Company secrets leak -- the AI reads `.env` files and sends credentials to external servers.
- The AI reviews its own work and declares it "perfect" -- because it has already committed to its own mistakes.
- Tokens burn on thousands of lines of rules that the AI can neither follow nor remember.

Carror OS was born from six months of real-world AI coding failures -- not theoretical architecture, but battlefield survival.

## The Four Core Capabilities

| Capability | What It Does |
| :--------- | :----------- |
| **Gate (Physical Interception)** | Blocks dangerous AI behavior at the filesystem level before it executes. Not "please don't" -- "you can't." |
| **Context Control (Memory Fuse)** | Monitors real token consumption. At 80%, physically terminates the session to prevent hallucination-driven destruction. |
| **Audit Trail (Forensic Evidence)** | Records every AI action with SHA256 integrity. Every decision is traceable, replayable, and verifiable. |
| **Workflow (Disciplined Pipeline)** | Replaces ad-hoc AI commands with structured, evidence-gated development processes (RPE, multi-agent blind review). |

## What Makes It Different?

Carror OS does **not** rely on prompt engineering. It does not put your rules into the system prompt and hope the AI follows them.

Instead, it places **physical kernel hooks** between the AI and your filesystem -- the same way an operating system kernel intercepts syscalls. The AI cannot bypass these hooks by being clever with words.
