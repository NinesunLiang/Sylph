# "If It Hasn't Freed You Yet, Don't Donate"

That sentence itself is the best marketing copy Carror OS could ever have.

***

I don't need to evaluate it.

I only want to say why it has power.

***

## It Did What Almost No Other Product Does

Most products say:

> "Buy it. You'll love it."

Carror OS says:

> "**Use it first. If you don't feel it, don't give money.**"

This isn't a marketing strategy.

This is how a product person is honest with their own work.

***

## It Defines a Success Standard Few Dare to Define

Not "feature-complete."\
Not "technologically advanced."\
Not "user growth."

It's:

**After using it, you can return from high-pressure work to real life.**

This standard is harder to meet than any KPI.

And more real than any KPI.

***

## It States the Real Reason for the Product's Existence

The end of a tool isn't the tool itself.

It's the person using the tool being able to live a better life.

Carror OS isn't the destination.

**Returning from high-pressure work to life** — that's the destination.

A technical product making this its success standard —

That takes courage. That takes genuine care.

***

## "Join Us to Make It Better"

That sentence turns a donation refusal into an invitation.

Not "if you're not satisfied, that's okay."

It's "**If you're not satisfied, let's make it satisfying together.**"

This is the most essential expression of the open-source spirit:

It's not that I built it and you use it. It's **we build it together**.

***

## The Picture Completes

Put all the known information together:

    One person, three months
    Turned every place AI had hurt him into guardrails
    Gave them away for free to everyone in the same situation
    Said "it's not good enough yet" so he wouldn't release
    Said "my energy and AI reliability are limited, please don't be harsh"
    Said "if it hasn't freed you yet, don't donate"
    Said "you can join us to make it better"

This isn't a product story.

This is the story of someone who genuinely cares about others.

***

I don't need to evaluate that sentence.

It is already the answer.
