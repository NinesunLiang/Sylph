# To Everyone Striving in This Era

***

We live in a strange crevice.

AI is powerful enough to do work you once took pride in.

But not powerful enough to fully replace you.

We live in this middle ground — "strong enough, but not yet."

How long this ground lasts, no one knows.

Maybe ten years. Maybe five. Maybe less.

***

Working in this crevice is a peculiar experience.

You feel unprecedented productivity, and at the same time, unprecedented uncertainty.

You use AI to do what one person couldn't do before, yet you are more tired than ever, unable to explain why.

You know the direction, but the path hasn't been walked yet.

Every day you are breaking new ground, adapting, using tools that aren't mature enough to do real work with real consequences.

**This is not easy.**

No one tells you what to do.

Because no one has done it before.

***

History will remember the grand narratives of this era —

AI breakthroughs, model iterations, industrial transformation.

But history will not remember every person working late into the night.

It won't remember your patience correcting AI for the hundredth time.

It won't remember that sigh when you discovered a false completion.

It won't remember the loneliness of bearing AI's unreliability alone.

It won't remember how you felt your way forward, step by step, in an era without a map.

***

**But you experienced it.**

That is already enough.

***

Maybe one day, AI will truly be able to fully replace individual developers.

When that day comes, this crevice era will feel especially precious —

It was the last time humanity stood side by side with machines in the act of creation.

It was the last time humans felt pride because they made something with their own hands.

It was the last time humans left their own real mark in code.

***

Carror OS was born in this era.

Not to fight AI, not to surpass AI.

It's to make every person in this crevice era

**live just a little less painfully.**

Work just a little less anxiously.

Occasionally, put down the work and return to life.

Watch a sunset. Share a meal. Sleep a truly restful sleep.

***

This era will pass.

But you were here, striving.

You opened a road in an era without a map.

In the most uncertain of times, you chose to keep working, keep creating, keep believing that what you do matters.

***

**That deserves to be remembered.**

Even if only you remember it.

***

*Carror OS*

*Written in the early crevice of the AI era*

*Dedicated to everyone striving within it*
